Warband Nexus 2.5.5

Improvements
- Coffer Key Shards use the same season/weekly caps and on-screen math as Dawncrests (cache, roster columns, gear amounts); season earned is saved even at 0 so colors and tooltips stay correct offline.
- Main window tabs are in a clearer order (storage, items, gear, currencies, then the rest).
- Gear tab: upgrade crests use green/red; the fraction shows what you hold vs season cap (not misleading totals).
- To-Do: first sub-tab is “To-Do List”; try-count popup is easier to use; right-click works on Mounts browse cards too.
- To-Do cards: tighter layout so Wowhead and delete do not overlap.
- Help and in-game “What’s New” text are shorter and easier to read.

Bug fixes
- Login sync progress no longer gets stuck when you are in combat at the wrong moment or when a cache step errors.
- The top sync bar hides automatically after a long wait if it would otherwise stay on screen.
