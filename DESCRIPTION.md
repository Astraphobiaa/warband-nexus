# ⚔️ Warband Nexus ⚔️
**Your entire Warband. One window.**

You play more than one character — maybe a dozen. Warband Nexus is built for that life: **one place to see everything your alts own, owe, and still need to do**, without logging in and out just to answer a simple question.

Type **`/wn`** and get a live picture of your whole account.

---

## Why download it?

If any of this sounds familiar, Warband Nexus is for you:

- You need a reagent **right now**, but you have no idea which alt still has it in the bank.
- You are not sure **which character is closest to a rep goal**, or whether that renown is account-wide.
- Vault week is ending and you **forgot which alts still have unclaimed rewards**.
- You found a BoE upgrade on your main and want to know **which alt can actually use it**.
- You are farming a rare mount and lost track of **how many tries** it has taken.
- You maintain crafters across the warband and want to **plan a craft before you swap characters**.

Warband Nexus keeps scanning in the background while you play. Open a bank once, log in on an alt once — the addon remembers and builds a **cross-character dashboard** you can search, sort, and act on from anywhere.

**Bottom line:** less alt-hopping, fewer spreadsheets, more time actually playing.

---

## What you can do with it

### See your warband at a glance
Check gold, item level, professions, keystones, rested XP, guild, and last played for every character. See **total gold across realms** without adding it up yourself.

### Find any item — on any character
Search bags, personal banks, Warband Bank, and guild storage from one window. Hover an item to see **who owns it and where it lives**. No more “I know I have it somewhere.”

### Manage gold with intent
Set per-character gold targets, move money with deposit/withdraw helpers, and review **Money Logs** so you always know where gold went.

### Make smarter gear decisions
Compare equipped gear and item levels, spot upgrade paths, and see **which alt has a useful BoE or Warbound piece** stashed away for your current character.

### Stay on top of weekly PvE
Review Great Vault progress, raid lockouts, Mythic+ scores, keystones, affixes, and dungeon history **for every alt** — not just whoever you are logged into.

### Track reps and currencies account-wide
Factions, renown, paragon, and friendship ranks in one view. Currencies grouped by expansion, with **per-character breakdowns on hover** so you know who to log in on.

### Plan crafts across your crafters
Profession skills, concentration, knowledge, specs, and weekly progress for all tracked characters. **Recipe Companion** shows which alt has which reagents (and quality tiers) while you have a profession window open.

### Set goals and actually finish them
Add mounts, pets, toys, achievements, and transmog to your to-do list. Keep the floating **Plans Tracker** on screen while you play. The built-in **try counter** remembers how long that farm really took.

### Celebrate drops — without losing context
Optional on-screen alerts for new mounts, pets, toys, and achievements, with try count when relevant. Themes, position, and sounds are yours to tune.

### Measure collection progress
Account-wide stats for mounts, pets, toys, titles, achievement points, and storage usage — useful whether you are a collector or just cleaning house.

## Built for everyday play

- **Automatic scans** on login and when you open banks — no manual refresh ritual.
- **Search on every tab** — filter fast when your warband is huge.
- **Chat reminders** for rep and currency gains, plus vault nudges when rewards are waiting.
- **Themes and layout options** so the UI fits your setup.
- **Minimap button** — left-click opens the hub, right-click toggles Plans Tracker.
- **Lightweight** — event-driven updates and caching, not constant polling.
- **11 languages** — EN, DE, ES, FR, IT, KO, PT, RU, ZH-CN, ZH-TW.

## Quick start

1. Install the addon.
2. Log into each character once (open banks when you can).
3. Type **`/wn`** and explore.

Your dashboard fills in as you play — the more you use your alts, the more complete it becomes.

| Command | What it does |
|---------|----------------|
| `/wn` | Open the main window |
| `/wn plan` | Toggle Plans Tracker |
| `/wn options` | Open settings |
| `/wn changelog` | What's New |
| `/wn help` | Full command list |
